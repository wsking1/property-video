The gap

Photos ask buyers to imagine.
Video shows them.

Most listings on Trade Me and Airbnb still run on static photos alone. It's not that agents don't know video helps — it's that the usual path to getting it is slow, expensive, and easy to put off.

Questions

Before you book

Is AI-generated video actually good enough for a real listing?+
The technology has moved fast — modern AI video tools generate genuine camera movement (push-ins, pans, reveals), not just a slideshow with pan-and-zoom. Every project includes a review step before delivery, so if something doesn't look right, it gets fixed before you ever post it.
What photos do I need to send?+
The same listing photos you'd already use for Trade Me or Airbnb — no new photoshoot required. More photos generally means more to work with, but even a standard 10–15 photo set is enough for a full video.
What if I don't like the result?+
Every package includes one round of revisions — music, pacing, or text changes are common requests and are included at no extra cost. If it's a bigger change, we'll talk it through directly.
How does payment work?+
Bookings are confirmed and paid via secure Stripe checkout, with fixed pricing shown upfront — no quotes, no hidden fees.
Do you work with Airbnb hosts as well as real estate agents?+
Yes — the same process applies whether you're marketing a property for sale or a holiday rental listing. Both benefit from the same walkthrough treatment.
Why should I book now if PropShots is brand new?+
Founding clients get direct, personal turnaround at a rate locked in before demand grows. Being early means more attention on your project, not less.
